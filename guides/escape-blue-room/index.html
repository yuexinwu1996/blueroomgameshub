FAQ

How strict is the 45 minute timer?

The timer pauses each time the narration interjects, giving you roughly five hidden minutes. Use them to reset props and confirm the cipher alignment before the vault segment.

Can we solo the vault puzzle?

Solo runs are possible but expect substantial backtracking. Duo play is the sweet spot because one person can manage the prisms while the other tracks ledger permutations.

What happens if we trigger the dart trap?

You lose the hint bonus and the systems force a 90 second blackout. Stick to mirrored input on the switchboard to avoid the penalty entirely.
